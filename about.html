Our Values

Committed

We are committed to giving a value for money service, providing professional, passionate staff who demonstrate a real love of teaching. We are committed to building strong personal relationships where compliance and safeguarding are paramount.

Open

We are committed to giving a value for We pride ourselves on our open-door policy. We encourage all communication as this strengthens links and builds partnerships. Encouraging our staff to discuss and reflect on their working practices is very important to us.

Respectful

SET Recruitment are respectful of the demands and pressures of our staff and clients. By listening to their needs, we understand what they want from us as an agency and ensure that everyone feels supported, valued and respected.

Enriching

We are committed to giving a value for We are dedicated to training our staff, looking after their wellbeing, and ensuring a flexible work-life balance. Enriching our staff will have a positive impact on the teaching and learning provided in our childcare and educational settings.
